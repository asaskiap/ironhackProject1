MUFFIN THE CAT'S ADVENTURES IN SPACE

🪐 Muffin The Cat is on a mission in outer space, far away from her home planet. She is here to hunt for the elusive SPACEFISH 🐟!

🪐 She must COLLECT 3 of them and make it home in time for DINNER.

🪐 On her way she will encounter many treacherous distractions in form of ferns 🌱, boxes 📦 and sunbeams - they will hinder her progress, so she must try to avoid them!

🪐 By collecting MUFFINS, Muffin The Cat will gain extra speed and dash ability [SPACE] for jumping over sunbeams.

🪐 She will also want to collect STARS for shooting power.

🪐 Before she can make it home with her loot, Muffin The Cat will encounter an evil OCTOPUS named Monday who wants to steal her fish. She can defeat him with her shooting power [Y]! But she must avoid getting hit herself, or Monday might succeed in taking her fish!

🪐 And Muffin must be sure to manage everything before the TIMER runs out!!
