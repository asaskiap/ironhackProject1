START GAME

Objectives:

You are stranded in outer space, far away from your home planet. You must make it home in time for dinner - but you can not come empty handed: collect at least 3 of the rare and elusive space fish 🐟!

On your way, you will find speed and gain shooting [Y] power by collecting stars.

Collect Muffins for extra dash ability [SPACE]: This will help you avoid the powerful sunbeams that will slow you down, by jumping over them.

Try to avoid the distractions posed by ferns 🌱 and boxes 📦 - they will hinder your progress!

When you are ready to go back home, a wormhole will appear to take you back, but it is guarded buy a powerful guard whom you must first defeat with the shooting power you have collected.

And be sure to manage everything before the TIMER runs out!!

Character controlls: 🞀 🞁 🞂 🞃 | dash: [space] | shoot: [Y]
